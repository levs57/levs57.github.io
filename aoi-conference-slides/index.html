Introduction

This talk is a bit spontaneous, so author asks for audience's forgiveness in advance.

Neither did I plan to talk here, nor was I doing a lot of math last year - due to the potentially scheduled end of world my focus moved to the more material planes of existence.

Therefore, it will only recast what I know in a form appropriate for the supremely learned audience; maybe adding one or two questions which haunted me during my journey through the Algebra of the Infrared - with the hope of maybe being useful to people currently capable and more adept in abstract matters.

Author would like to thank Andrei Losev for initiating this line of research, Yan Soibelman, Mikhail Kapranov for countless hours of discussions and actually explaining me some parts of the bigger picture, and Gregory Moore for inviting me to speak here.

With that being said, let's start; but not before publicly denouncing the war of aggression and encouraging everyone to try to change the world for better.
